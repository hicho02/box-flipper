### Features

### How to install
1. Go to the **Releases** section in this repository.
2. Download the `box_flipper.fap` file.
3. Connect your Flipper Zero to your PC.
4. Using **qFlipper**, copy the `.fap` file into the `applications/Games` folder.
5. Enjoy!

### Controls
* **OK:** Punch.
* **Left / Right:** Dodge.
* **Back:** Exit game.

### Gameplay
Watch your opponent closely. When they flash, they are about to punch. **Dodge!** If you dodge at the right time, the enemy will become vulnerable (an indicator will appear above their head). That's your window to land your punches.
